# Ingredients for making Rava Dosa

Rava (Sooji)
½ cup

Rice Flour
½ cup

Maida
¼ cup

Onion
1 small, chopped

Green Chillies
1-2, finely chopped

Ginger
½ inch, finely chopped

Pepper
½ teaspoon, crushed

Cumin Seeds
½ teaspoon

Curry Leaves
8-10, chopped

Butter Milk
2 cups (300 ml)

Water
½ cup (If required)

Coriander Leaves
2 tablespoons, chopped

Salt
To taste

Oil
For roasting
